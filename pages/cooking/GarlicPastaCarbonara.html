Garlic Pasta Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 200g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Cook Spaghetti:

    2. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Sauté Garlic and Pancetta:

    3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and diced pancetta. Sauté until the pancetta is crispy and the garlic is fragrant.

  3. Prepare Carbonara Sauce:

    4. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese to form the carbonara sauce.

  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce:

    5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with garlic and pancetta. Pour the carbonara sauce over the pasta. Toss until the pasta is coated in the sauce.

  5. Season:

    6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Continue tossing until the pasta is well-coated and heated through.

  6. Garnish:

    7. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for added freshness.

  7. Serve:

    8. Serve the Garlic Pasta Carbonara warm. Enjoy!
