Tinapa Aglio Olio Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup tinapa flakes (smoked fish flakes)
  • 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley for garnish

Procedure:

  1. Cook Spaghetti:

    2. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Sauté Garlic:

    3. In a large pan, heat extra-virgin olive oil over medium heat. Add thinly sliced garlic and sauté until golden and fragrant.

  3. Add Tinapa Flakes:

    4. Add tinapa flakes to the pan and sauté for a few minutes until heated through.

  4. Toss with Spaghetti:

    5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with garlic and tinapa. Toss until the spaghetti is well coated.

  5. Season:

    6. Season with salt to taste. Add red pepper flakes if you want a bit of heat.

  6. Garnish:

    7. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley for added freshness.

  7. Serve:

    8. Serve the Tinapa Aglio Olio warm. Enjoy the unique and flavorful dish!
