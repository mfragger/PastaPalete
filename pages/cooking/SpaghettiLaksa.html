Spaghetti Laksa Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 400ml coconut milk
  • 500ml chicken or vegetable broth
  • 200g cooked chicken, shredded
  • 200g prawns, peeled and deveined
  • Bean sprouts, lime wedges, and fresh cilantro for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure:

  1. Prepare Laksa Paste:

    In a food processor, combine lemongrass, shallots, garlic, ginger, red chillies, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and shrimp paste. Blend until you get a smooth paste.

  2. Cook Laksa Paste:

    Heat vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the Laksa paste and sauté for 5-7 minutes until fragrant.

  3. Cook Spaghetti:

    In a separate pot, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  4. Combine Laksa and Broth:

    Pour in coconut milk and chicken or vegetable broth into the pan with the Laksa paste. Bring the mixture to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes.

  5. Add Protein:

    Add shredded chicken and prawns to the Laksa broth. Cook until the prawns are pink and opaque.

  6. Season:

    Season the Laksa broth with salt and pepper to taste.

  7. Assemble:

    Place a portion of cooked spaghetti in serving bowls. Ladle the Laksa broth, chicken, and prawns over the spaghetti.

  8. Garnish:

    Garnish with bean sprouts, lime wedges, and fresh cilantro.

  9. Serve:

    Serve hot and enjoy your Spaghetti Laksa!
